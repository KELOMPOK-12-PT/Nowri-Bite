About Nowri-Bite

Who We Are

Nowri-Bite was born out of a simple observation: busy people often compromise on their meals. Students skip lunch to study, and office workers settle for unhealthy snacks to meet deadlines. We decided to change that by combining the practicality of Japanese and Korean convenience food with wholesome, delicious ingredients tailored to local tastes.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of practical, healthy, and affordable Asian-inspired daily meals for young professionals and students across the nation, making every busy day a little bit tastier.

Our Mission

  • Provide ready-to-eat meals that are effortless to consume on the go.
  • Maintain strict hygiene standards from preparation to packaging.
  • Keep prices affordable without ever compromising on ingredient quality or portion size.
  • Continuously innovate our menu to bring exciting new flavors to our customers.
